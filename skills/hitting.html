Hitting Fundamentals

Learn How to Hit (Spike) in Volleyball

Hitting — or spiking — is where the magic happens. It’s the big finish to your team’s play, and the ultimate weapon to score points with power and style!

Types of Hits

  • Power Spike: A strong downward shot aiming for the floor.
  • Roll Shot: A softer attack that aims over blockers.
  • Tip: A light touch over the net to catch the defense sleeping.

Steps for a Proper Volleyball Hit

  1. Start with an approach: step-right-left (or left-right for lefties).
  2. Jump explosively with both arms swinging upward.
  3. Pull your hitting arm back like a bowstring.
  4. Strike the top of the ball with an open hand at the highest point.
  5. Follow through with your arm down across your body for control.
