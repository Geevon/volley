Blocking Fundamentals

Learn How to Block in Volleyball

Blocking is your first line of defense! Done right, a good block can **shut down an attack** before it even crosses the net. Plus — let’s be real — nothing feels better than stuffing a hitter!

Types of Blocks

  • Single Block: One player jumps to block the ball alone.
  • Double Block: Two players coordinate to cover a wider area.
  • Triple Block: All three front-row players form a wall at the net (beast mode!).

Steps for a Proper Volleyball Block

  1. Stand close to the net with knees bent, ready to jump.
  2. Watch the hitter's approach and eyes closely.
  3. Jump straight up (not forward!) when they jump to hit.
  4. Reach high with hands spread wide over the net.
  5. Press your hands over the net to "close the door" and block the ball.
