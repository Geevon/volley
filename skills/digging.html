Types of Digs

  • Forearm Dig: Classic dig using the forearms, same platform as passing.
  • Dive: Sacrifice your body to the floor to save a hard hit ball (legend move!).
  • One-Handed Dig: Emergency reach with one arm to keep the ball in play.

Steps for a Proper Volleyball Dig

  1. Stay low with your knees bent and weight forward on your toes.
  2. Keep your platform ready ‚Äî hands together and arms straight.
  3. Watch the hitter closely and anticipate where the ball is going.
  4. Move quickly and position your body behind the ball.
  5. Use your legs to absorb the impact, NOT your arms ‚Äî control is key!
